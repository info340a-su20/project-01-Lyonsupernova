About

In light of the COVID-19 Pandemic, schools are mainly operating online and zoom meetings become the norm. However, since there is no uniform rule of organization, different instructors often organize these zoom links very differently, making it hard for students to quickly navigate. Our website is built to help solve this problem.

Our Design

We enable the creation of innovative products, services, and communications that address society’s most pressing issues, including environmental sustainability. Accessibility design, to better assists users who are in need, we have included accessibility designs such as adding arial labels to help with better screening reading. Responsive Design, We have also added responsive designs to fit the needs of different screen size.

Our Approach

Lyons was in charge of the structure, styling and functionality of the two main pages. Wayne was in charge of the structure, styling, and functionality of the navigation bar. Our Goal

“Progress isn't made by early risers. It's made by lazy men trying to find easier ways to do something.” ― Robert Heinlein
We aim to provide a clear structured and easy to use platform for people who rely on zoom meetings for school, work, or other activities. Our goal is to allow the users to edit, change, and access their zoom events anywhere and anytime without having to navigate into nested pages to find these links. In this way, our website clients will be more efficient on organizing zoom links, planning ahead, and checking the deadling status.

Our Features

  1. We have designed two different views to best fit our users’ need. One calendar view and one category view.
    2. Category view - allows the users to easily organize different zoom links with their customized categories Calendar view - helps the users to organize their events in chronological order.
  2. In both views, the users can easily edit/add/remove events
  3. Sort chronologically
  4. Add new event / delete new event
  5. two views — calendar and category view better organize
